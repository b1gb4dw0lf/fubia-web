Privacy Policy for Fubia

Last Updated: 12.01.2023

KaBa Tech ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Fubia mobile application (the "App"). This privacy policy outlines our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App, in accordance with Google Play Store requirements.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of personally identifiable information:

Account Information: We collect information provided during account creation, specifically email address and username.
Communication Data: We may process communication data, including any communication you send to us.
Profile Data: This includes your username, in-game achievements, and other profile-related information.
Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the App, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
Usage Data

We automatically collect information on how you interact with the App:

Game Progress: We collect data related to your progress in the game, such as scores, currency, bonuses and levels completed.
Analytics: We use third-party analytics tools to collect and analyze usage patterns, including frequency of use, features accessed, and performance metrics. This helps us improve the App's functionality and user experience.
Payment Information

If you make in-app purchases, your payment information is processed directly by the app store and not collected or stored by us.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Provision: To provide and maintain the App, including user accounts, customer support, and personalized features.
Analytics: To analyze user behavior and improve our App's functionality, content, and user experience.
Communication: To communicate with you regarding updates, and important announcements related to the App.
Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent: We may share your information if you have given us explicit consent to do so.
Service Providers: We may engage third-party companies and individuals to facilitate the App's features and performance, and these service providers may have access to your personal information.
Your Control over Data Collection

Users have the option to exercise control over the information collected by adjusting their device settings or by contacting us directly. However, it is imperative to note that declining to consent to this data control statement may impede the full enjoyment of the App. Given its integration with the Google Play Store, acceptance of this statement is deemed necessary for seamless functionality.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective when posted on this page. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or within the App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
